Asgard's best Lasagna

Look at this fine piece of lasagna!

This is lasagna is so good even the gods eat it.

Give it a try and you'll understand why!


Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Fry the meat
  2. Fry the onion
  3. Add tomato
  4. Layer in a deep dish tray
  5. Cook in oven at 200 degrees celsius for 20 minutes
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving
